# Welcome to the AWS Student Builder Group at IIT
The official AWS Student Builder Group at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT). As part of a global network spanning universities in more than 60 countries, we operate in direct affiliation with Amazon Web Services (AWS).
## Our Mission
Our mission is to foster a collaborative and inclusive learning environment where students can explore, build, and innovate using cloud technologies. We focus on AWS technologies, professional topics, and hands-on learning to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical industry demands in areas such as DevOps, cloud infrastructure, and cybersecurity.
## Membership
Membership is completely voluntary, free, and highly inclusive.
The group is open to students from all years of study and any major or degree at IIT who are 18 years of age or older.
Participation is free from discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, or any other legally protected status.
