# Welcome to the AWS Cloud Club at IIT

The official AWS Cloud Club at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT). As part of a global network spanning universities in more than 60 countries, we operate in direct affiliation with Amazon Web Services (AWS). 

## Our Mission
Our mission is to foster a collaborative and inclusive learning environment where students can explore, build, and innovate using cloud technologies. We focus on AWS technologies, professional topics, and hands-on learning. Our goal is to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical industry demands in areas such as DevOps, cloud infrastructure, and cybersecurity. 

## Membership

Membership is completely voluntary and highly inclusive. 

The club is open to students from all years of study and any major or degree at IIT who are 18 years of age or older. 

Participation in the AWS Cloud Club at IIT is completely free for all students. 

No annual membership fee shall be charged from any member. 

Membership and participation in Club activities shall be free from discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, or any other legally protected status.
